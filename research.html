Research

My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Explainable AI, Reinforcement Learning, and Computer Vision, with a strong focus on computational chemistry applications such as drug discovery, QSAR modeling, and molecular docking.

Core areas

  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning for structured and molecular data
  • Explainable AI (XAI) for model interpretation and decision support
  • Reinforcement Learning for optimization and sequential decision-making
  • Computer Vision for medical and scientific imaging tasks

Computational chemistry applications

  • Drug discovery pipelines: data curation, modeling, and evaluation
  • QSAR modeling: feature engineering, benchmarking, and validation
  • Molecular docking: ligand–target interaction analysis and workflow automation
